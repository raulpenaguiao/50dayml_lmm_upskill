# Day 38: MLOps Basics with MLflow - Further Reading

This day covers MLflow, a platform for managing machine learning experiments, tracking metrics, versioning models, and simplifying the ML lifecycle.

## References

1. **MLflow Official Documentation**
   - [MLflow Docs](https://mlflow.org/)
   - Complete documentation covering tracking, models, registry, and projects.

2. **MLflow Tutorial and Getting Started**
   - [MLflow Quick Start](https://mlflow.org/docs/latest/quickstart.html)
   - Official tutorial for getting started with experiment tracking and model management.

3. **Experiment Tracking Best Practices**
   - [Weights & Biases: Experiment Tracking](https://docs.wandb.ai/)
   - Comprehensive guide to tracking experiments with alternatives to MLflow.

4. **Model Registry and Versioning**
   - [MLflow Model Registry](https://mlflow.org/docs/latest/model-registry.html)
   - Official guide to model versioning, staging, and transition management.

5. **Building ML Pipelines**
   - [MLflow Projects](https://mlflow.org/docs/latest/projects.html)
   - Guide to packaging ML projects with reproducible environments and parameters.
